About

Located off the Nanny Goat Strut, behind Decca Restaurant in the heart of NuLu, DREAMLAND is a 120-person black-box theater with adjoining bar, lounge, and gallery space. It is a venue dedicated to exploring the nexus of progressive art forms, filling a much-needed gap in the Louisville arts community. We are committed to promoting experimental and innovative music, dance, film and visual art forms. Our smaller size has allowed for engaging experiences, and our intimate setting brings the patrons closer in on the action.
